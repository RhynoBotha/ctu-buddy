Frequently Asked Questions


Are full qualifications accredited?

Answer : Yes, all full qualifications offered by CTU Training Solutions are accredited by relevant South African authorities, including the Council on Higher Education (CHE), the Department of Higher Education and Training (DHET), and the relevant Sector Education and Training Authorities (SETAs).

Can I study part-time for a full-qualification?

CTU Training Solutions offers part-time study options for selected full qualifications. Part-time study allows students to balance their education with work and personal commitments while still acquiring the necessary skills and knowledge for their chosen field. To check if a specific qualification has a part-time option, please refer to the course page on our website: Academic Qualifications -- Click Here

What is an occupational qualification ?

An occupational is a credential closely connected to a specific trade, occupation or profession, obtained through work-based learning experiences
